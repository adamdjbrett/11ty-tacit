<section class="page">
  <h1>About</h1>

  <p>Tacit is a minimal Eleventy starter that uses the <a href="https://yegor256.github.io/tacit/">Tacit CSS</a> typographic stylesheet. It keeps defaults simple while still looking polished, and is ready for blogging with RSS and sitemap out of the box.</p>

  <ul>
    <li>Built with Eleventy 3.1.2 (ESM)</li>
    <li>Styled with Tacit CSS 1.9.5 (unminified in <code>/css/tacit.css</code>)</li>
    <li>Date handling uses Luxon to avoid Nunjucks date parsing errors</li>
  </ul>

  <p>Edit content inside <code>content/</code> and place static assets in <code>public/</code>.</p>
</section>
